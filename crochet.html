Crocheting

Crochet is more than just a hobby for me—it's a form of meditation and creative expression. I love working with different yarns and patterns to create everything from simple scarves to complex amigurumi figures. The repetitive motions help me relax while producing something beautiful and functional.

Handmade Crochet Blanket

I made a crochet blanket for my mom, pouring time and care into every stitch. I loved crocheting it because it was so relaxing, the yarn was wonderfully soft, and it felt extra special knowing I was creating it for someone I love.

Small Crochet Projects

A collection of my crochet projects includes pouches, bags, and little figures that I’ve stitched together with creativity and care. I love making them because each piece feels unique, playful, and full of personality, showing just how fun crochet can be.

Gallery

A collection of my work and interests

Handmade Crochet Blanket

I made a crochet blanket for my mom, pouring time and care into every stitch. I loved crocheting it because it was so relaxing, the yarn was wonderfully soft, and it felt extra special knowing I was creating it for someone I love.

The Folk of the Air by Holly Black

Holly Black’s The Folk of the Air series is a dark and enchanting fantasy that plunges readers into a world of cunning faeries, court politics, and dangerous beauty. I love it because of the twists, betrayals, and morally complex characters, which make the story feel as thrilling as it is magical.

The Apothecary Diaries

The Apothecary Diaries is a historical mystery that follows Maomao, a witty apothecary who unravels palace intrigues with her sharp mind and knowledge of medicine. I love it because it mixes clever mysteries with the richness of a historical setting, making every chapter feel like solving a puzzle in another world.

Small Crochet Projects

A collection of my crochet projects includes pouches, bags, and little figures that I’ve stitched together with creativity and care. I love making them because each piece feels unique, playful, and full of personality, showing just how fun crochet can be.

The Land of Stories by Chris Colfer

Chris Colfer’s The Land of Stories is a magical book series that reimagines classic fairy tales through an adventurous, modern twist. I love it because of its imaginative world-building and how it makes familiar fairy tales feel exciting, fresh, and full of wonder.

Corpse Bride

Tim Burton’s Corpse Bride is a gothic fairytale brought to life through enchanting stop-motion, weaving together dark charm and bittersweet beauty. I love it because of its stunning animation and storyline, which make the story feel magical and unforgettable.

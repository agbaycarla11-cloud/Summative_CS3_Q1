Reading Books

Books have always been my gateway to different worlds and perspectives. I enjoy a wide range of genres, from classic literature to fantasy, and I particularly love stories that challenge my thinking or transport me to new places. Reading not only enriches my vocabulary but also enhances my understanding of human nature and society.

The Folk of the Air by Holly Black

Holly Black’s The Folk of the Air series is a dark and enchanting fantasy that plunges readers into a world of cunning faeries, court politics, and dangerous beauty. I love it because of the twists, betrayals, and morally complex characters, which make the story feel as thrilling as it is magical.1

The Land of Stories by Chris Colfer

Chris Colfer’s The Land of Stories is a magical book series that reimagines classic fairy tales through an adventurous, modern twist. I love it because of its imaginative world-building and how it makes familiar fairy tales feel exciting, fresh, and full of wonder.

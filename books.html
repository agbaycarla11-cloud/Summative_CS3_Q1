Personal Interests

Exploring my passions and hobbies

Crochet

Crochet is more than just a hobby for me—it's a form of meditation and creative expression. I love working with different yarns and patterns to create everything from simple scarves to complex amigurumi figures. The repetitive motions help me relax while producing something beautiful and functional.

Reading

Books have always been my gateway to different worlds and perspectives. I enjoy a wide range of genres, from classic literature to fantasy, and I particularly love stories that challenge my thinking or transport me to new places. Reading not only enriches my vocabulary but also enhances my understanding of human nature and society.

Shows

I love watching shows because they blend storytelling, visuals, and performance to create immersive worlds I can lose myself in. Whether it’s a lighthearted series or something more serious, I enjoy analyzing character development, cinematography, and narrative choices that make each story unique. What makes it even more meaningful is how shows can reflect different cultures and perspectives, helping me understand the world better while still giving me a space to relax, be entertained, and feel inspired.

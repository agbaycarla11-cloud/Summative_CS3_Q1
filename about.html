About the Author

Get to know me better

Lian Carla Agbay

Hi there! My name is Carla Agbay.

I’m a student with a passion for creativity and storytelling. In my free time, you’ll often find me crocheting cozy projects, diving into a good book, or enjoying a great movie. These hobbies inspire me to see the world in different ways—through imagination, artistry, and emotion.

This website serves as a platform to share my journey, showcase my interests, and connect with others who share similar passions. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me and my work.

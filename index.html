About Me

I am a student who's heart is split between a few different loves: the intricate art of crochet, where a single strand of yarn can become something beautiful and cozy; the colorful, expressive world of painting; and the endless adventure of reading, which opens up new worlds and ideas with every chapter. This personal space is where I share my creative journey and the small joys that shape who I am becoming.
